Canvas Quiz Uploader

This Addon allows you to upload quiz questions to your Canvas courses. It uses a personal or school-configured access code so you don't have to copy and paste materials.

To connect, go through the Setup steps in the Addon Menu.

After you connect...

Once you connect your account, the Addon will automatically pull from your Canvas courses and quizzes. Questions are uploaded to existing quizzes, so you need to create the Quiz in your course first. This may be added as an improvement in the future.

If you run into problems, you can request help by emailing the developer or filing an issue.
